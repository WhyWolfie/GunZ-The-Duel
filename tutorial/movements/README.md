# GunZ Tutorial for SSBM (Melee) Players

**Slash shot** is the wavedash of GunZ  
**Half step** is the dash dance  
**Reload shot** is the L cancel  

If you like Melee and shooters, you'll like GunZ.

**Leading is gone** – aim straight at opponents.  

**Rebinds**: Block → Right Click, Alt Fire → Shift.  

## Beginner

- **Timed dashes**: Dash > Dash  
- **Buffered bunny hop**: Jump > Jump (buffered)  
- **Bhop dash**: Jump (buffered) > Dash  
- **Wall run**: Run at wall > Jump (Dash into it + Jump for extra)  
- **Wall cancel**: Jump at wall > Jump (buffered)  
- **Vertical wall run**: Run into wall > Jump  
- **Slash animation cancel**: Locked animation > Slash  
- **Wall climb**: (Air) Dash > Slash > Jump (buffered)  
- **Flip cancel**: Mash space when flipped  
- **Butterfly**: Jump > Dash > Slash > Block  
- **Switch shot**: Shoot > Switch > Shoot  
- **Slash shot**: Jump > Dash > Slash > Switch > Shoot (buffer shot)  
- **Wall post**: Slash shot into wall > Jump (buffered)  
- **Gear tap**: Jump > Slash > Switch > Dash > Shoot  
- **Light step**: (Air) Dash > Slash > Dash  
- **Fast med**: Run into wall > Med 1 > Switch > Med 2 > Switch  
- **Turtling**: Hold Block  
- **Foot shot**: Shoot feet  
- **Massive charge**: Hold M1  
- **180 massive**: Jump > Dash > Slash > Turn 180  
- **Wall hang**: Jump > Alt fire  
- **Half step**: Slash shot > Dash (gun out)  
- **Reload shot**: Shoot > Reload > Switch > Shoot  
- **Reload slash shot**: Slash shot > Reload  
- **Reload gear tap**: Gear tap > Reload  
- **Reload half step**: Half step > Reload  

## Intermediate

- **Half half step**: Slash shot > Jump > Dash (gun out)  
- **Reload half half step**: Half half step > Reload  
- **Dash break**: Fast half step (high air)  
- **Flash step**: Jump > Dash > Slash > Switch Switch  
- **Fast wall climb**: Flash step into wall > Jump (buffered)  
- **Double dash flash step**: Flash step > Dash  
- **Static Double butterfly**: Jump > Slash Block > Slash Block  
- **Double butterfly**: Jump > Slash Block > Dash > Slash Block  
- **Stun block**: Mash block in massive stun  
- **Quick flip**: Alt fire > Block  
- **Instafall**: Quick flip > Jump > Slash  
- **Instakill**: Instafall > Switch > Reload shot  
- **Instafall (gun)**: Quick flip > Switch > Shoot > Reload  
- **Insta spray**: Quick flip > Switch > Spray  
- **Insta sword**: Quick flip > Double butterfly > Drift > Repeat  
- **Sky block**: Block looking up  
- **Sky massive**: Massive straight up  
- **Ground block**: Block looking down  
- **Ground massive**: Massive straight down  
- **Void step**: Grounded slash (hold) > Charge 1 frame  
- **Range slash**: Slash turtle at range  
- **Turn slash**: Slash > Turn 90  
- **Corpse slash**: Aim down downed > Slash  
- **Block rush**: Dash > Flip > Block  
- **Slash stop**: Land > Slash (ground) > Block  
- **Re-block**: Block after hit > Move  
- **Forbidden step**: Static reload slash shot > Reload half step  
- **Mansion reload**: Reload > Vertical wall run > Drift back > Jump > Drift forward > Jump  
- **Cry tech**: Bind cry easy  
- **Wall hang animation cancel**: Wall cancel > Wall hang > Dash  

## Advanced

- **BF hang**: Jump > Slash > Block > Wall hang  
- **Static triple butterfly**: Jump > Slash Block x3 (250 FPS)  
- **Triple butterfly**: Jump > Slash Block x2 > Dash > Slash Block  
- **Silent double flash step**: Jump > Slash > Switch Switch > Dash > Repeat  
- **Late dash**: Shoot > Reload > Sword > Dash  
- **Late dash half step**: Reload slash shot > Sword > Dash  
- **Triple dash double flash step**: Static flash > Dash x2 > Flash > Dash  
- **1 shot double half step**: Static flash > Dash > Late dash half step  
- **Double half step**: Late dash gear tap > Late dash half step  
- **Extended air nade**: Slash shot > Bhop > Look up 75° > Throw  
- **Ghost evade / flip evade**: Turn during recoil  
- **Magic switch**: Ghost evade > Switch to gun  
- **Bi-TBF** / **GDHS**: See description videos  

## Common Problems & Tips

- **Can't switch fast**: Avoid Q/E (long cooldown)  
- **Butterfly / slash shot block/gun late**: Slash *after* dash  
- **Wall climb flip**: Slash to stop; don't mash  
- **Gear tap roll**: Slash up or dash too late  
- **Reload shot slow**: Shoot+Reload+Switch same time  
- **Static DBF slash late**: Don't mash; time slow  
- **Reload half step hard**: Rebind to mouse  
- **Hands hurt**: Build stamina  
- **Instafall fail**: Minimize flip-to-slash time  
- **TBF impossible**: Block on mouse? 250 FPS? Quick tap block  
- **Aim bad**: Good hardware/settings; disable enhance pointer
